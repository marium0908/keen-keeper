# 👥 KeenKeeper — Keep Your Friendships Alive

KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management tool designed to help you maintain and nurture your most meaningful connections. In a busy world, it's easy to lose track of time; KeenKeeper ensures you never drift apart from the people who matter most.

## 🚀 Features

- **Personal Friend Shelf**: A visual dashboard of your friends, categorized by their contact status.
- **Smart Reminders**: Automatic tracking of days since last contact with visual cues for friends who need attention.
- **Interaction Timeline**: A chronological history of your calls, texts, and meetups.
- **Social Analytics**: Insights into your connection patterns and social health.
- **Quick Check-Ins**: Log interactions with a single click to keep your timeline up to date.
- **Custom Goals**: Set individual contact frequency goals for each friend.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: React 19, TypeScript, Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS 4, shadcn/ui
- **Animations**: Motion (formerly Framer Motion)
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Charts**: Recharts
- **Notifications**: Sonner

## 📱 Responsive Design

KeenKeeper is fully responsive and optimized for:
- **Mobile**: Easy logging on the go.
- **Tablet**: Clear overview of your social circle.
- **Desktop**: Detailed analytics and management.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The app uses a **Deep Forest Green** palette (`#1a4731`) to evoke a sense of growth, stability, and nurturing—perfect for tending to the "garden" of your friendships.
